no paper tickets - only passport required for boarding

You don’t get tickets in Russian railways (unless you print them at home). Train stewards will check their tablets and let you in. Just take a passport with you, it is impossible to board the train without a passport.

But anyway we will send you an official Russian railroad PDF ticket so you could print it.
