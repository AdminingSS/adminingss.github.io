Meet Russian Trains

There are different types of trains in Russia, connecting major cities - from the fastest Sapsan, Allegro and Swift high-speed trains to the slower regular, double-decker and luxury overnight trains. High-speed trains are the best choice if you want to get to your destination as fast as possible. Luxury overnight trains are great, if you are looking for an experience - these trains between the capitals and Trans-Siberian route remind about Orient Express. Double-deckers and Regular trains are cheaper but still comfortable and convenient option
